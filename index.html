Molecule Visualizer

Explore two complementary experiences: a stylised Three.js scene and an interactive molecule rendered with 3Dmol.js. Use the controls in each viewport to orbit, pan, and zoom around the structures.

  • Drag with the left mouse button to orbit the object.
  • Hold the right mouse button to pan the camera.
  • Scroll to zoom in and out.
